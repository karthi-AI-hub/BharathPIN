Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 3, 2025

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

NexGen Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BharathPIN mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("BharathPIN") and related services.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Types of Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Search Queries: PIN codes, post office names, and location data you search for
  • Favorites and Bookmarks: PIN codes and post offices you save as favorites
  • User Preferences: App settings and customization preferences
  • Feedback and Communications: Any feedback, reviews, or support requests you submit

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, frequency of use, and session duration
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, app version, and technical logs
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics for app improvement
  • Location Data: Approximate location (if you grant location permission) to provide relevant search results

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third-party services such as:

  • Analytics providers for app performance monitoring
  • Government databases for postal information updates
  • App stores for download and usage statistics

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide information or grant permissions
  • Legitimate Interest: To provide and improve our services, ensure security, and analyze usage
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the core functionality of the app
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Service Provision: To provide accurate PIN code and post office information across India
  • Personalization: To save your favorite searches, provide search history, and customize your experience
  • App Improvement and Analytics: To analyze usage patterns, improve app performance, and develop new features
  • Technical Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and troubleshoot issues
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and ensure app security
  • Communication: To send important app updates, security alerts, and service notifications
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Research and Development: To conduct research for improving postal services accessibility

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers and Business Partners

  • Cloud Hosting Services: For data storage and app infrastructure
  • Analytics Providers: For app performance monitoring and usage analytics
  • Technical Support: For app maintenance and customer support services
  • Payment Processors: If applicable for premium features (all providers are PCI DSS compliant)

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and data protection requirements.

4.2 Legal Requirements and Safety

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court orders, or government requests
  • Safety Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public
  • Fraud Prevention: To investigate and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities
  • Terms Enforcement: To enforce our terms of service and policies

4.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.4 Aggregated and De-identified Data

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information for research, marketing, or other business purposes.

5. Data Storage, Security, and Retention

5.1 Data Storage Locations

  • Local Storage: Your favorites, search history, and app preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Some data may be stored on secure cloud servers located in India and other countries
  • Backup and Sync: Optional data synchronization across your devices (with your explicit consent)

5.2 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+ and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms for our systems
  • Security Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats and vulnerabilities
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure Development: Secure coding practices and regular security code reviews
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the data necessary for app functionality
  • Employee Training: Regular security training for all personnel with data access

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 2 years after deletion
  • Search History: Stored locally until manually deleted or app is uninstalled
  • Technical Logs: Retained for up to 12 months for security and performance monitoring
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we have about you
  • Right to Rectification: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Right to Restriction: Request limitation of processing of your personal information
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal information
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@bharathpin.com. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

6.3 App Permissions and Controls

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch real-time PIN code and post office data
  • Storage: To save your favorites and search history locally on your device
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity and provide offline features
  • Location (Optional): To provide location-based search results (you can deny this permission)
  • Notifications (Optional): To send app updates and service notifications

You can manage these permissions through your device settings at any time.

6.4 Data Export and Deletion

  • Export Data: You can export your favorites and search history from within the app
  • Clear Data: You can clear your search history and favorites from the app settings
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to permanently delete your account and associated data

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

BharathPIN is designed for general use and is suitable for users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without parental consent.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our servers.

Parents and guardians can:

  • Review and delete their child's personal information
  • Refuse to permit further collection of their child's information
  • Contact us to request information about our data practices regarding children

8.1 Government Data Sources

We integrate with official Indian postal service databases and government APIs to provide accurate and up-to-date information. These integrations are subject to the respective government privacy policies.

8.2 Analytics and Performance Monitoring

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For app usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Firebase: For crash reporting and app performance metrics
  • App Store Analytics: For download and usage statistics

8.3 External Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8.4 Social Media Integration

If you choose to share content through social media platforms, such sharing is governed by the privacy policies of those platforms.

9. Analytics, Advertising, and Marketing

9.1 Analytics

We use analytics services to understand how users interact with our app and to improve user experience. These services collect anonymous usage data including:

  • App usage patterns and feature popularity
  • Performance metrics and crash reports
  • User engagement and retention statistics
  • Technical diagnostics for troubleshooting

9.2 Advertising

Currently, BharathPIN does not display third-party advertisements. If we introduce advertising in the future, we will:

  • Update this privacy policy with detailed information
  • Provide opt-out mechanisms for personalized advertising
  • Ensure compliance with advertising privacy regulations
  • Only work with reputable advertising partners

9.3 Marketing Communications

We may send you marketing communications about new features, updates, and promotional offers. You can opt out of these communications at any time through app settings or by contacting us.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

10.1 Retention Periods

We retain different types of data for different periods:

  • User Account Data: While your account is active and up to 2 years after deletion
  • Search History: Stored locally on your device until manually deleted
  • Favorites and Bookmarks: Stored until you remove them or delete the app
  • Technical Logs: Up to 12 months for security and performance monitoring
  • Support Communications: Up to 3 years for quality assurance and legal compliance
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data may be retained indefinitely in anonymized form

10.2 Automatic Deletion

Some data is automatically deleted:

  • Temporary files and cache are regularly cleared
  • Session data is deleted when you close the app
  • Error logs are automatically purged after 12 months

10.3 Manual Deletion

You can manually delete:

  • Individual search history entries
  • Saved favorites and bookmarks
  • All app data by uninstalling the application
  • Your entire account by contacting our support team
